<WrappableParagraph title="Memories Specific Music Rules">
- Your submission must be entirely original in its entirety, and you must hold the rights of your composition. No partial arrangement of any series should be present in your submission.
- Your track needs to be at least 2 minutes long, and cannot exceed 7 minutes.
-Your track must be within the style aimed for VOLTAGE. Here are some genres/keywords that may help you.
    - Modern Drum and Bass
    - Bass House
    - Trap
    - Hard Techno / Rave Inspirations
    - Dubstep
    - Riddim
    - Intense Sound Design
    - We greatly recommend you to share your piece to the supervisor and check the inspiration playlist to know if it would fit.
</WrappableParagraph>
